Task #2 – Header & Navigation Premium Modernization
What & Why

Modernize only the Header & Navigation of the imported OCS OORJA website (artifacts/ocs-oorja) to a premium, world-class standard suitable for a serious Indian clean-energy manufacturer.

The Header should immediately communicate trust, engineering excellence, and professionalism while remaining clean, lightweight, and highly usable.

The Header is the second most important section after the Hero because it appears on every page and strongly influences the first impression.

Prioritize clarity, hierarchy, usability, and brand consistency over decorative effects.
